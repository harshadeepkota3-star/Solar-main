Get the power of the sun

In the sun-abundant landscape of Anantapur, we recognized an opportunity to transform how homes and businesses access energy. Starting as a focused solar solutions provider, we have evolved into a comprehensive power management company through passion, fair practices, and unwavering commitment to quality. With 10,000+ successful installations across Andhra Pradesh, MN Enterprises has proven its excellence in delivering solar and power solutions that perform reliably in the diverse power conditions that exist across our region.
